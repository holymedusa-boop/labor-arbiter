### Why Costs Crashed So Fast

**1. Open-source disruption**
硅基智能's open-source **HeyGem** project (13,000+ GitHub stars) gave developers free tools to create digital humans. Instead of guarding the technology, they gave it away—capturing the ecosystem.

**2. Cloud-native architecture**
Modern AI avatars run on shared GPU clusters. The marginal cost of rendering one more avatar approaches zero.

**3. Competition intensity**
With over **110,000** digital human companies registered in China (per Tianyancha data), price competition is brutal.

**4. Model efficiency gains**
新一代多模态大模型 can generate talking heads from just **1 second** of video or a single photo—no studio required.

## The Technical Stack: How Modern Avatars Work

### Architecture Overview

Today's AI avatars aren't simple video loops—they're real-time generative systems.

| Component | Technology | Performance |
|-----------|-----------|-------------|
| **Face Generation** | Diffusion models + GAN | 4K resolution, 60fps |
| **Lip Sync** | Wav2Lip + custom models | <50ms latency |
| **Voice Synthesis** | Neural TTS + voice cloning | 98% similarity |
| **Body Movement** | Motion capture + AI prediction | Natural gestures |
| **Emotion Engine** | Multimodal LLM | Real-time expression |
| **Language** | Real-time translation | 20+ languages |

*Technical specifications from 硅基智能 DUIX ONE and industry benchmarks*

### The 1-Second Revolution

The most disruptive technical advance: **单图/1秒视频克隆**.

In 2023, creating a digital twin required:
- 5-30 minutes of studio video
- Professional lighting and green screen
- Manual cleanup and editing
- 2-7 day turnaround

In 2026, it requires:
- **1 photo** or **1 second** of casual video
- Any background, any lighting
- **30 seconds** processing time
- Instant deployment

This technological leap is what enabled the mass-market explosion.

- Data retention limits for facial training data
